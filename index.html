The Industry-Led Standard

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a premier training and deployment hub for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We specialize in regulated, high-consequence environments where precision is non-negotiable.


By bridging the gap between UK university research and real-world industrial projects, we ensure our practitioners are equipped with both theoretical depth and tactical agility.

Academic Partnership

Our curriculum is co-developed with leading UK universities to integrate emerging AI governance and risk modeling directly into our training pipeline.
